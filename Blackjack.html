Basic Rules

  • Objective:Beat dealer without exceeding 21
  • Card Values:Number cards = Number on card, face card = 10 Ace = 1 or 11
  • Deal:Player gets 2 cards, dealer gets 2 one facing up
  • Player turn:Hit = get another card stand = Keep current hand
  • Dealer Turn:Reveal card,hit until 17+, then stand.
  • Winning/Losing:Beat dealer without exceeding 21, bust if over.
